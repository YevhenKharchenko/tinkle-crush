About

Tinkle Crush - Match 3 RPG is a colorful and dynamic puzzle RPG where players match gems to unleash attacks and conquer fantasy enemies. Developed by twinklematch.com, this game features strategic hero building, magical battles, and hundreds of challenging levels.
Whether you're a fan of puzzles or RPGs, Tinkle Crush brings a perfect mix of both genres into one epic experience.
